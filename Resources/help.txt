Adventure Tracker is a simple application designed to use minimal battery allowing you to send location updates to the tracker server without running your phone battery flat.<br><br>

<b>How To Track Your Position in 2 easy steps</b><br>
Step 1:<br>
Press the Start Tracking button, if you have a previous tracking session you will be asked if you would like to <i>resume your previous session from your last location</i> or <i>start a new session</i><br><br>
Step 2:<br>
Use the Email Location button to send a link of your trail to yourself or people who you would like to follow you.<br>
